Drumming

Playing the set

My first drumset had paper heads and I got it when I was around 4 years old. I loved it. I figured out how to play with songs on my own. I ended up getting my first real drumset when I was in 6th grade.

My first show

My first show playing the drums was in 6th grade. I played in front of the high school. There was a sound issue so I played a drum solo. I was nervous, but just wanted to play.
